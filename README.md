Personal academic website of **Rojin Zandi**, PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Northeastern University.

🌐 **Live site:** [rojinzandi.github.io](https://rojinzandi.github.io)

## About

I research multimodal sensing, WiFi-based perception, and deep learning for activity recognition. My work focuses on fusing radio-frequency signals (WiFi Channel State Information) with other sensing modalities to enable robust, non-intrusive recognition of human and robotic activities.
