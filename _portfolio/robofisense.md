Attention-based framework for robotic arm activity recognition using WiFi Channel State Information. Designed a multi-head attention architecture that processes amplitude and phase information from CSI signals to classify robotic activities without cameras or wearables.

**Published in:** IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Signal Processing (JSTSP), 2024
