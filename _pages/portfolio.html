--- permalink: /portfolio/ title: "Portfolio" --- ## Research Projects ### RoboFiSense Attention-based framework for robotic arm activity recognition using WiFi Channel State Information. Designed a multi-head attention architecture that processes amplitude and phase information from CSI signals to classify robotic activities without cameras or wearables. ### RoboMNIST A large-scale multimodal dataset for multi-robot activity recognition, combining WiFi CSI, video, and other sensing modalities. Published in Nature Scientific Data (2025) and designed to serve as a benchmark for the multimodal sensing community. ### Phase-Aware Deep Fusion (ICASSP 2026) Novel fusion method that goes beyond amplitude-only CSI features by incorporating phase information through a dedicated deep fusion architecture.
